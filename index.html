What we do

Three ways we work with you.

Our own open-source products you can run yourself or let us host — plus bespoke development and the infrastructure to run it, from the cloud down to the physical office.

Open-source products

Open source · self-host or SaaS

Technologies we build with every feature free and nothing behind a paywall. Run them yourself for free — or let us host and operate them as a managed SaaS.

  • Full-featured, genuinely open source
  • Free to self-host, your data stays yours
  • Managed SaaS when you'd rather not operate it
  • Advanced support & SLAs, always optional

Custom development

Bespoke · open or closed source

Software built and operated around your project rather than ours — pragmatic architecture, clean code, and documentation you can actually read.

  • Custom applications, APIs & tooling
  • Web & backend systems
  • CI/CD, monitoring & reliability
  • Open or closed source — your call

Infrastructure

Design · build · operate

The systems your software runs on — designed, deployed and operated for you, all the way from the cloud down to the racks and the office network.

  • Infrastructure design & architecture
  • Cloud & self-hosted setups we run for you
  • Custom rack & datacenter design
  • Office networking — wiring, Wi-Fi & access control

What we stand for

Software should be honest.

LiDiSoft grew out of frustration with opaque software and hidden paywalls. Our principles are a direct answer to that.

Transparent

Open code and open roadmaps. You can see how things work and where they're going.

No paywalls

Every feature ships free. We never lock functionality behind a subscription.

Yours to own

Self-hosting is always free. Your data and your deployment stay under your control.

Reliable

We favour stable, well-operated systems over hype — built to keep running.
